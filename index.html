What that means DOJO?

A dōjō is a hall or place for immersive learning or meditation. This is traditionally in the field of martial arts, but has been seen increasingly in other fields, such as meditation and software development. The term literally means "place of the Way" in Japanese.

Coding dojo

“A Coding Dojo is a meeting where a bunch of coders get together to work on a programming challenge. They are there to have fun and to engage in Deliberate Practice in order to improve their skills.” ― Codingdojo.org

DOJO Club in stockholm

The DOJO CLub in Stockholm meets every week, two times presential and two times online, with DOJO sections dedicated to CSS, Javascript, and Python. A presential Sunday DOJO section is dedicated to first-time visitors and consists of cool CSS challenges. Come over any section and double-check it. It is entirely free. Welcome!
