Presential DOJO!

  • It takes place every Thursday and Sunday in room number 3 on the third floor at Kungsholmens bibliotek Internationella biblioteket - Stockholms stadsbibliotek
  • Address: Sankt Eriksgatan 33, 112 39 Stockholm
  • The Sundays DOJO section is dedicated to CSS challenges and first-time participants. So, sign up and let us know if it is your first time in a DOJO.
  • Just ask for directions to our dojo at the reception library, and don't forget: Shiiii! We are in a library!
  • IMPORTANT: Due to COVID restrictions, you must present your vaccinal passport at the library entrance.

Remote dojo
